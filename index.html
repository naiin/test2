Create Mini Game

The core functionality of LiveDuel allows you to create customizable mini-games, in the form of polls, quizzes, and prediction competitions. Mini-Games can be easily embedded on any website through the simple copy/paste of your own unique LiveDuel code. LiveDuel’s interactive content will increase engagement, time spent on website, and allow you to capture real digital currency, such as; email address, and cookies. The result of which can be used to enable email marketing, custom audience advertising, digital advertising, and retargeting.

Fan interface

  • Your mini-game will be embedded on any website you chose.
  • Fans pick their answer through a simple click or swipe.
  • Fans then have the option to register with your brand – allowing you to capture real data, and activate your localized or global fan base.
  • Each game can be customized, using your own imagery.

Results/League Table

LiveDuel gives you the option to create prediction competitions, with a league table format. Fans can compete against each other for points, and ultimately prizes, strengthening their relationship with your brand. Long-term competitions will keep your fans coming back to your website.

Analytics Dashboard

LiveDuel’s easy to use analytics dashboard allows you to A/B Test competitions, monitor your subscribers / fans, and download the email addresses of each fan who has connected with your brand through LiveDuel.
